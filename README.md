# Executive Summary

This project analyzes marketing campaign performance across multiple channels and geographic regions using Power BI.

The dashboard provides actionable insights into campaign ROI, revenue generation, marketing spend efficiency, and regional performance.

---

# Business Problem

Marketing teams invest significant budgets across multiple advertising channels but often struggle to identify which campaigns generate the highest return on investment.

This dashboard helps answer:

- Which marketing campaigns generate the highest ROI?
- Which regions deliver the highest revenue?
- How efficiently is marketing budget being utilized?
- Which marketing channels should receive future investment?

# Key Insights

### Best Performing Campaign

Influencer Marketing delivered the highest ROI and emerged as the top-performing campaign.

### Highest Revenue Region

Africa generated the highest revenue among all regions.

### Marketing Effectiveness

Digital marketing channels accounted for the majority of campaign activity.

### Revenue Optimization

Campaign ROI analysis highlights opportunities for better budget allocation across channels.

---

# Business Recommendations

- Increase investment in Influencer Marketing campaigns.
- Optimize underperforming traditional marketing channels.
- Expand successful campaigns into high-performing regions.
- Continuously monitor ROI for budget allocation decisions.
